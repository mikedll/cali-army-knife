dub
==============

Like du, but sorts your output by size. Compare:

### du

    > du -h -d 1 
      0B	./Colloquy Transcripts
     14G	./Library
     23G	./Personal
    673M	./Work
     37G	.


### dub:

    > dub
          37.0G .
          23.0G ./Personal
          14.0G ./Library
         673.0M ./Work
           0.0B ./Colloquy Transcripts

## options      

    -c Enable colorized output.
